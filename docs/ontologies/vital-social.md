## Purpose

Social media and messaging models: posts, messages, profiles, campaigns,
and related edges.

## Key classes

- **`DirectMessage`** — a direct message between users.
- **`CampaignAction`** — campaign/marketing action definitions.
- **`Edge_hasCampaignAction`** — connecting campaigns to actions.
- Various campaign filter, document, and social profile classes.
